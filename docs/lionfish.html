Why we have to erradicate the fish Lionfish?

It´s important to eradicate this species due to its invasive, predatory and territorialist characteristics and behavior. The lionfish is native to the Indian Ocean and the Western Pacific.

The presence of the lionfish in the Panamanian Caribbean represents a great threat, as it has a voracious appetite, can eat almost anything and has rapid reproduction mechanisms.

The impact in the Panamanian Caribbean is direct, since it feeds on small fish, mollusks and crustaceans, a coral ecosystem is believed to be capable of being cleaned by 90% and in 45 minutes up to 30 fish can be eaten, disturbing the ecosystemic balance.
